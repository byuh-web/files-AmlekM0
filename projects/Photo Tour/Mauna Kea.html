Mauna Kea

Mauna Kea is a dormant volcano located on the Big Island. It is the highest point in Hawaii and offers stunning views of the surrounding landscape. Visitors can hike to the summit, visit the Mauna Kea Observatories, or simply enjoy the breathtaking scenery.

Mauna kea is the only place on the island where it snows. So if your tired of the heat this should be your next destination.
