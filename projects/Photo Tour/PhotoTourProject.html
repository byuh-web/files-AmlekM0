Welcome to Hawaii!!

Hawaii is a beautiful place to visit. It has many different attractions and activities to offer. Whether you are looking for a relaxing beach vacation or an adventurous hiking trip, Hawaii has something for everyone.

    Hilo

    Hilo is a charming town located on the eastern side of the Big Island. It is known for its lush rainforests, stunning waterfalls, and vibrant culture. Visitors can explore the beautiful Akaka Falls, take a stroll through the Liliuokalani Gardens, or visit the Hilo Farmers Market to experience the local flavors and crafts.

  • Hilo

    • Kona

    Kona is a city located on the western side of the Big Island. It is known for its coffee plantations, beautiful beaches, and rich cultural heritage. Visitors can explore the historic town of Kailua-Kona, visit the Puuhonua o Honaunau National Historical Park, or take a boat tour to see the famous Big Island coastline.

    • Volcano National Park

    Volcano National Park is a UNESCO World Heritage site located on the Big Island. It offers stunning views of active volcanoes, diverse wildlife, and unique geological formations. Visitors can hike through the park's trails, visit the Kīlauea Iki Crater, or take a guided tour to learn about the area's volcanic history.

  • Volcano National Park

    • Waipoio Valley

    Waipoio Valley is a scenic valley located on the Big Island. It is known for its lush vegetation, beautiful waterfalls, and rich cultural heritage. Visitors can explore the valley's trails, visit the Waipoio Falls, or take a guided tour to learn about the area's history and traditions.
